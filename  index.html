Population Growth in the United States

By Jared Craig

Source: Our World in Data, World population by region.

Just 200 hundred years ago the population of the world was slightly over a billion. 200 years ago the United States had just 9.6 million people populating its boarders. Today, the US has a population of 329 million. The population of the US has contiously grown since 1950. However, the growth rate has gone up and down from 1950 to 2020.
